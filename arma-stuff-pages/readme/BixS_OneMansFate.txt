Campaign One Man's Fate
~
Story
~
Former member of the RACS, sergeant Paul Clark,  is enjoying his retirement in his native village Hunapu. War is so far not in his concern but everything will change soon. The village is under a raid from Sahrani Liberation Army (SLA) and they shoot dead everyone in the village. Paul manage to escape and because of his revenge he decides to rejoin the RACS. However, his witness of murder is not given much attention and so he has no choice but to join the fight and fill his revenge through the war, which is raging at Sahrani right now.

Important figures
~
Paul Clark - Player's character, sergeant of RACS
Tom Smith - Paul's friend from South Sahrani
